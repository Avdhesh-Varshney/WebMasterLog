as a result of normal wear. Do not attempt to charge a wet iPhone; refer to the user guide for cleaning and drying instructions. Liquid damage is not covered
under warranty.
2. The display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design, and these corners are within a standard rectangle. When measured as a standard rectangular
shape, the screen is 15.54 centimetres / 6.12 inches (Phone 14 Pro), 17.00 centimetres / 6.69 inches (iPhone 14 Pro Max) diagonally. Actual viewable area is less.
