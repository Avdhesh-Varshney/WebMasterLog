What does World Population means?

World Population refers to the total number of people living on the planet Earth at a given point in time, regardless of their geographic location or other demographic characteristics.

  • Protective measures during sex

    Safe sex is sexual activity using methods or contraceptive devices (such as condoms) to reduce the risk of transmitting or acquiring sexually transmitted infections (STIs) ,especially HIV.

  • Family planning

    Family planning is the information, means and methods that allow individuals and couples to decide if and when to have children. This includes a wide range of contraceptives such as pills, implants, intrauterine devices, surgical procedures that limit fertility as well as non-invasive methods like the calendar method and abstinence.
