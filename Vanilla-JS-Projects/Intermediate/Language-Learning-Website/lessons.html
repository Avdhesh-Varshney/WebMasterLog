Lesson 1: Basic Vocabulary

Learn the basic words and phrases you need to start speaking the language.

  • Hello - Hola
  • Goodbye - Adiós
  • Please - Por favor
  • Thank you - Gracias

Lesson 2: Common Phrases

Practice common phrases used in everyday conversations.

  • How are you? - ¿Cómo estás?
  • I'm fine, thank you. - Estoy bien, gracias.
  • What is your name? - ¿Cómo te llamas?
  • My name is... - Me llamo...
